Fly Drone to GPS Coordinate

Note: Make sure the coordinates are within the drone's flying range and comply with local regulations.

Instructions

  1. Enter the GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) of the destination.
  2. Specify the desired altitude in meters (default is 5 meters).
  3. Set the heading in degrees (0-359, default is 0 degrees).
  4. Click "Start Mission" to begin the flight.
  5. You will be directed to a page showing the mission progress.
  6. After completion, you can download captured images as a zip file.
